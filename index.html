THE "FEED DETECTIVE"

FRONTEND AUTOMATION

Project built with:
HTML, CSS, Java-Script, PHP, Ajax

At Visual Meta we have to synchronize millions of products from our partner-shops on a daily basis in order to provide users with the most recent product information (price changes, availability or even new products). Prior to allowing new product feeds to join the synchronization schedule, they have to undergo a detailed quality check. A manual validation of CSV & XML files with hundreds of thousands of lines is very time consuming and vague. To ease the burden of this huge effort, we planned, developed and released the “Feed Detective”, a web-based solution to automate & simplify this process.
